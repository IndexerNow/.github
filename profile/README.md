## Our Products

### ⚡ IndexerNow
**Submit URLs to Google, Bing & IndexNow. Get cited by AI.**

IndexerNow pushes your URLs to Google, Bing & IndexNow in one click — then shows whether AI assistants can crawl, read, and cite your site. Includes a 0–100 AI discoverability score, real AI-crawler tracking, indexability audits, bulk submission, sitemap support, and CSV export. Uses your own Google Search Console account. Free daily quota forever.
